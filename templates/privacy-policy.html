PRIVACY POLICY- 998-P

1. Definitions

For the purpose of this Policy:

a. "Add-in" means any third-party or proprietary software extension or plugin provided by 998-P that integrates with other software platforms (e.g., Microsoft Word) to provide additional functionalities to Users.

b. "Aggregated Data" means compiled or summarized information derived from multiple Users' data, used for statistical, analytical, or research purposes, without identifying any individual User.

c. "Anonymized Data" means any information collected through the Platform or Surveys that has been stripped of personally identifiable information such that the User cannot reasonably be identified, either directly or indirectly.

d. "Consent" means the explicit, informed, and freely given agreement of a User to allow 998-P to collect, store, process, and use their Personal Information or Survey Data for the purposes outlined in this Policy.

e. "Data Processor" means any third-party service provider engaged by 998-P to process, store, or analyse User Data on behalf of the Company, including hosting providers, cloud services, payment gateways, AI service providers, and analytics or marketing vendors.

f. "Effective Date" means the date on which official permanent registration numbers are rolled out, triggering the validity period for associated vouchers and related services.

g. "Permanent Registration Number" means the official identification number issued to Professionals after verification and completion of all formal registration procedures. This number replaces any Temporary Registration Number and serves as a long-term identifier within the 998-P Platform.

h. "Platform" means the website, digital interfaces, surveys, communication channels, mobile applications, Add-ins, and all services provided under the brand 998-P.

i. "Professional(s)" means individuals or entities using the Platform for purposes of registration, participation in surveys, engagement with services, or any other interaction facilitated by the Platform.

j. "Services" means all products, features, tools, resources, and functionalities offered by the Platform, including but not limited to survey participation, access to professional networks, AI tools, analytics, and Add-in functionalities.

k. "Survey Data" means all responses, inputs, opinions, feedback, or other information provided by Users through surveys, questionnaires, research instruments, or any other data collection mechanism administered by the Company.

l. "Temporary Registration Number" means the provisional identification assigned to Users prior to the formal issuance and rollout of official professional registration numbers.

m. "Third Party" means any individual, entity, or organization other than 998-P that may process, store, or otherwise have access to User Data in accordance with this Policy, including affiliates, subsidiaries, partners, service providers, or government authorities.

n. "User(s)" means any person who accesses, registers, or interacts with the Platform, including Professionals, survey participants, and visitors.

o. "Voucher" means the incentive issued by the Company to Users who participate in designated surveys, currently valued at INR 5,000, subject to the terms and conditions set forth in this Policy and as communicated to Users at the time of issuance.

p. "Communication Channels" means any email, SMS, push notifications, messaging platforms, or other modes of interaction through which 998-P communicates with Users.

q. "AI Tools" means proprietary or third-party artificial intelligence-powered functionalities integrated within the Platform or Add-in that assist Users in performing tasks, generating content, or analysing data in accordance with 998-P services.

r. "Platform Content" means all materials, templates, guidance, educational resources, or information available on the Platform, including text, graphics, audio, video, software, and interactive elements.

s. "User Data" means all data, content, or information generated, uploaded, or shared by Users while using the Platform, including Personal Information, Survey Data, prompts, documents, files, queries, and profile information.

t. "Payment Gateway Providers" means third-party financial services companies engaged by 998-P to process payments, transactions, or vouchers, while ensuring compliance with applicable laws and security standards.

2. Scope

This Policy applies to all Professionals who register on the Platform, as well as those who fill or submit surveys administered by the Company. It further governs every interaction undertaken with the Company in connection with 998-P.

However, it must be noted that this Policy does not extend to any third-party websites or services that may be linked to the Platform, as such external platforms are governed by their own privacy practices and terms.

3. Data of Minors

The Platform and its Services are not intended for individuals below eighteen (18) years of age. We do not knowingly collect or process Personal Information from minors. If it is discovered that such information has been inadvertently collected, it shall be promptly deleted.

4. Information Collected

The Company may collect various categories of information from Professionals and Users accessing the 998-P Platform, including Personal Information, Survey Data, Technical Information, and Transactional Information. Personal Information may comprise your name, contact details, professional qualifications, areas of practice, and other identifiers necessary for providing the Services. Survey Data includes any responses, opinions, or feedback voluntarily submitted through surveys or research forms administered by the Company. Technical Information may consist of IP addresses, browser type, device identifiers, and access logs, while Transactional Information—where applicable—refers to limited payment-related details processed through third-party payment gateways.

Our practices and procedures for the collection, use, and storage of such information are designed to ensure the secure and efficient use of the 998-P Platform and associated Services. When You interact with the Platform through the web interface, Add-in, emails, telephone calls, or other correspondence, we may request that You voluntarily provide information that personally identifies You or could be used to identify You. By doing so, you consent to the collection and use of such information by the Company in accordance with this Policy.

998-P does not collect Personal Information without Your explicit consent. Once consent is provided, we may use the information shared by You solely for the purpose of providing the subscribed Services. Information that is anonymized but directly or indirectly associated with Personal Information may also be treated as Personal Information.

The types of information we may collect include, but are not limited to: Your name; contact details such as email address and phone number; demographic data, including date of birth and postal code; information regarding Your usage of the Services; limited and anonymized financial information arising from transactions, such as the last four digits of bank accounts, UPI IDs, or credit/debit card numbers as partially exposed to the Company by Payment Gateway Service Providers in compliance with applicable laws and regulations; information related to Your profession, career, domain of expertise, consultation fees, services or products offered, work experience, and other professional details; and any other information You voluntarily provide, including images, documents, or files shared through emails, letters, or surveys.

Additionally, we may collect information when You provide feedback, modify Your email preferences, respond to surveys, or otherwise communicate with the Company, to help improve our Services and maintain accurate records.

5. Purpose of Collection, Storage, and Processing of Your Data

We collect, store, and process Your Personal Information and User Data only where a lawful basis exists, such as Your explicit consent or where it is necessary for Our legitimate interests in order to provide, administer, and improve the Services offered through the 998-P Platform. The primary purpose of processing Your data is to verify Your identity, enable smooth registration, and facilitate Your use of the Platform as a Professional or User. Information collected is further used to communicate with You via email, notifications, or other approved channels regarding updates to the Platform, professional opportunities, new features, and important changes to these Terms.

We may also use data to personalise Your experience, generate relevant recommendations, provide promotional material consistent with Your interests, and respond to queries, complaints, or bugs reported by you. Additionally, we process data to detect, prevent, and investigate fraudulent, illegal, or prohibited activities, and to ensure compliance with Our obligations under law.

Importantly, 998-P does not sell, rent, or lease Your Personal Information to third parties, nor do We use Your queries, files, or documents to train or re-train any AI systems or models. Any data You provide is only ephemerally processed to generate the requested outputs or services, and Our systems do not permanently retain or repurpose this information for model training or commercial exploitation.

Where We engage trusted third-party service providers or API partners to support the functioning of the Platform, we ensure that they meet industry-standard privacy safeguards and do not use Your data to improve their own systems or models. Your files, prompts, outputs, and professional information remain fully confidential and are not monitored or accessed by Us, except where strictly necessary to maintain or secure the Platform.

By using the 998-P Platform, you acknowledge that Your information is collected and processed solely for enabling the delivery, enhancement, and lawful administration of the Services and not for any secondary or unrelated commercial purposes.

5.1 Legal Basis for Processing:

We process your Personal Information and User Data based on one or more of the following lawful bases:

  1. Your explicit consent;
  2. The necessity of processing for the performance of a contract or pre-contractual obligations;
  3. Compliance with legal obligations;
  4. Legitimate interests pursued by the Platform, such as improving services, ensuring security, and preventing fraud; and
  5. Protection of your vital interests or those of another person, where applicable.

We may also disclose Your information if required to comply with a court order or legal directive, to enforce Our Terms of Use, to protect Our rights and property, to ensure the safety of Users or the public, or to seek professional advice under legally compliant agreements.
