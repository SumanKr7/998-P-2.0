2. What type of professionals is 998-P designed for?

Under the current mission, it is designed for the regulated commercio-legal professionals, i.e., Chartered Accountants, Cost and Management Accountants, Company Secretaries, Insolvency Professionals, Lawyers and Valuers.

As more professionals utilize the platform, the AI system continuously learns and improves, refining its performance and capabilities.

5. How do I get work on 998-P?

You receive project opportunities either through bidding or round-robin allocation (fair rotation system).

6. What is Round Robin allocation?

It’s a transparent method to equally distribute work among eligible professionals without bias.

7. What is the bidding process?

You bid for assignments by quoting your price, timeline, and experience. The best value provider gets selected.

8. Can freshers get work here?

Yes. There are special listings and filters to promote newer professionals to get small but real assignments to build trust.

9. Will I get notified of new opportunities?

Yes, you’ll get real-time alerts via email, in-app, and WhatsApp/ SMS (if enabled).

10. How are standard rates decided?

In the beginning, the platform will analyze the bids received and accepted to set a benchmark rate. The system will suggest a standard price range based on data.
