What is 998-P?

998-P is a smart, AI-powered platform built to help professionals manage their work, collaborate with others, and grow their practice.

2

Who is 998-P for?

It is designed for Insolvency Professionals (IPs), CAs, CSs, Lawyers, and other service providers.

3

How is 998-P different from a regular ERP system?

998-P is built specifically for professionals with AI features, compliance workflows, and an integrated marketplace to access real work opportunities.

4

Is this a marketplace or a workflow tool?

It’s both. You can get new opportunities and also manage your complete workflow in one place.

5

How do I get work on 998-P?

You receive project opportunities either through bidding or round-robin allocation (fair rotation system).

6

What is Round Robin allocation?

It’s a transparent method to equally distribute work among eligible professionals without bias.

7

What is the bidding process?

You bid for assignments by quoting your price, timeline, and experience. The best value provider gets selected.

8

Can freshers get work here?

9

Will I get notified of new opportunities?

Yes, you’ll get real-time alerts via email, in-app, and WhatsApp/ SMS (if enabled).

How are standard rates decided?

In the beginning, we’ll analyze the bids received and accepted to set a benchmark rate. The system will suggest a standard price range based on data.

11

How does AI help me here?

AI helps you draft, compute, summarize, schedule, interpret documents, and suggest improvements in real-time.

No. AI assists you to be faster, better, and more efficient. You stay in control.

Can AI prepare reports and letters?

How accurate is the AI?
