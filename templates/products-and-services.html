What We Offer

Drives

These are Profession-Centric Workspaces

Drives are tailored, AI-assisted work environments built for specific professionals—Insolvency Professionals, Chartered Accountants, Company Secretaries, Lawyers, and more.
Each Drive streamlines the full scope of work relevant to that profession, including:

  • Automated task flows
  • AI-powered data computation & interpretation
  • Drafting of reports, notices, and compliance documents
  • Client correspondence and follow-ups
  • Real-time dashboard insights & reporting

Our Drives are not just tools—they're intelligent assistants that adapt to your work style, boost precision, and free up your time for high-value tasks.

Drive (IP:)

For Insolvency Professionals

Manage your entire insolvency lifecycle—CIRP, Liquidation, or Pre-Pack—with an intelligent system built specifically for IPs. Drive (IP:) automates public announcements, claim verifications, CoC formation, voting mechanisms, timelines, and IBBI filings.
It integrates compliance tracking, document drafting, correspondence logs, and even report preparation—all with AI assistance.

Focus on strategy and stakeholder management while your Drive handles the operational storm.
Everything you need to run a resolution or liquidation smoothly—centralized, structured, and scalable.

Drive (CS:)

For Company Secretaries

Drive (CS:) is your smart governance partner. Automate your secretarial calendar, from drafting meeting notices and resolutions to maintaining statutory registers and filing forms with MCA.
Set up multi-client dashboards, auto-reminders, compliance checkpoints, and AI-drafted board minutes. No more missed filings or manual tracking.

Whether you're managing a startup or a listed company, Drive (CS:) ensures you stay sharp, compliant, and ahead of the curve.
Simplify routine work and elevate your secretarial practice.

Drive (CA:)

For Chartered Accountants

Drive (CA:) transforms how you manage audits, taxation, and client accounts. Automatically generate task lists, manage statutory due dates, reconcile GST/IT returns, and auto-draft key communications.
Its AI layer assists with data interpretation, financial summaries, and alerts for anomalies or compliance gaps.

Juggle multiple clients without juggling your peace of mind.
Whether you’re a solo CA or a midsize or large firm, this Drive becomes your backbone for delivery, compliance, and credibility.

Drive (Lawyer:)

For Legal Professionals

Drive (Lawyer:) centralizes your legal operations—case timelines, client briefs, documentation, billing, and communication.
Use AI-assisted research tools to cut down on manual digging, auto-generate draft petitions, maintain client-wise folders, and never miss a court date again.

Smart, secure, and scalable—so you can focus on what matters: arguments, strategy, and winning cases.
From litigation to corporate advisory—run your practice effectively.

Modules

Built for Precision. Powered by Independence.

Not every professional task begins with a full-fledged project or workflow. Sometimes, you just need a powerful tool—quick, focused, and efficient. Our Modules are designed exactly for that. Each module is a standalone powerhouse crafted to perform specific functions—whether it's compliance tracking, automated document drafting, communication, or reporting.
Plug into what you need, skip what you don’t. No process dependencies, no long setups—just high-performance tools at your fingertips.
Explore our modules to unlock speed, accuracy, and control—on your own terms.

Meetings Module

Conduct, record & comply—seamlessly.

No more fragmented emails, missing minutes, or last-minute scrambles. The Meetings Module empowers you to handle every aspect of meetings—right from sending out notices, setting the agenda, managing attendance, to auto-generating compliant minutes aligned with respective Standards. Whether it’s CoC, Board, Shareholder, or Internal Review Meetings, it ensures structure, transparency, and real-time collaboration among participants.

Your one-stop tool to run error-free, compliant, and productive meetings, every time.

E-Voting Module

Secure. Transparent. Tamper-proof.

Digitize your decision-making with confidence. Our E-Voting Module is built for absolute integrity—conduct voting through encrypted links, set clear cut-off times, auto-generate resolution-wise reports, and ensure traceable audit trails for every vote cast. It’s ideal for committee approvals, shareholder consent, or any decision needing formal backing. No paper, no confusion—just clarity and compliance.

When every vote matters, this module guarantees trust and transparency.

VDR Module

Confidential data, shared smartly.

Handle sensitive documents with absolute control. Our VDR Module gives you a secure, structured environment to host, share, and monitor data. Be it for insolvency due diligence, M&A deals, legal reviews, compliance audits, and all other important usage—set permission levels, expiry timelines, view-only rights, and monitor user activity with ease. Version control and activity logs ensure transparency without compromising on confidentiality.

Now no more confusing emails, mismatched formats, and follow-up fatigue. From legal notices in insolvency to company disclosures, to all formal and informal announcements, make every publication accurate, timely, and traceable. The module provides pre-formatted templates based on your requirements and law type, integrated publisher contact lists (with language & region filters), and auto-alerts for publishing timelines. Maintain logs, track publication status, and even manage proof of publishing for regulatory records.

Turn what used to be a compliance headache into a process that runs like clockwork.

This Litigation Module offers an intuitive interface to record case details, court calendars, filing deadlines, legal team assignments, and associated documents. Compliance Module

Your dynamic compliance tracker.

Whether you’re a Company Secretary, IP, CFO, or entrepreneur, regulatory compliance is your non-negotiable responsibility. This Compliance Module uses a smart checklist engine to identify applicable laws based on your entity type, jurisdiction, and business nature. Stay ahead of every deadline, reduce legal exposure, and manage your compliance proactively.
