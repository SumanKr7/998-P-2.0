Thank You, Professionals Program

Rewarding Your Expertise!

A Note of Gratitude

We sincerely thank you for showing up and contributing your valuable time, knowledge, and perspective to the professional community. At 998-Platform, we believe that every professional deserves recognition, not only for their expertise but also for the role they play in shaping a better ecosystem.

The ‘Thank You, Professionals Program’ is our way of expressing appreciation — by rewarding your participation, referrals, and contributions while also creating avenues for you to influence, guide, and benefit from the platform you are helping us build.

Thank You 1 Survey Participation Reward

By completing the 998-P survey, and upon cross-verification of the information provided, you will receive a Win-Voucher worth ₹5,000.

This Win-Voucher:

  • Is non-transferable and non-cashable.
  • Can be redeemed against future subscription plans of 998-P.
  • Remains valid for 3 years from the date of issue.

This reward is a token of thanks for your input, which helps us shape the future of professional services.

Thank You 2: Survey Referral Rewards

Your network deserves to benefit too, and we want to thank you for helping us grow responsibly. For every professional you refer who successfully completes the survey and enters your Temporary Registration Number (TRN) in the ‘Referred By’ field while taking the Survey, not only the referred professionals receive their Survey Participation Reward, but you also receive the Survey Referral Reward, a Win-Voucher worth ₹1,000 for every ‘Successful Referral’.

These Win-Vouchers:

  • Are non-transferable and non-cashable.
  • Can be redeemed against future subscription payments on 998-P.
  • Remain valid for 3 years from the date of issue.

Missed or Incorrect Referral Entries?
If your referred professional forgets to mention your Temporary Registration Number (TRN) or wrongly mentions a different TRN, they can email us at desk4pros@998-p.com with your correct TRN and their details, to get the miss/ mistake corrected. Rest assured, we will ensure you receive the credits you rightfully deserve.

Thank You 3: Referral Credits

We appreciate your efforts in creating your network and building your contacts. Whenever your Successful Referral subscribes to any of the Module(s) and/ or Drive(s) on the 998-P in at least the next 7 years (may get extended to life-long), you will receive 10% of the subscription fee collected from that professional as Referral Credits.

These Referral Credits:

  • Are transferable.
  • Can be redeemed against future subscription payments on 998-P.
  • Can be encashed directly into your bank account.
  • Remain valid for 3 years from the date of issue.

This ensures a sustainable passive income stream — even during a sabbatical.

Thank You 4 Showcasing Windows

Your knowledge has the power to influence the future of the profession. We invite you to join us in honorary positions across our Domain-Specific Committees, Industry-Oriented Groups, and Training Circles.

Opportunities include:

  • Contributing to policy dialogues by assisting State and Central Governments in your specialized domain.
  • Serving on 998-P committees that guide industry practices, sectoral advancements, and technical upgradations.
  • Acting as mentors, trainers, or guest faculty members in professional education programs, including potential collaborations with Universities and Institutes.
  • Acting as a delegate/ expert contributor/ moderator/ panelist in subject meetings.

Please write to us on desk4pros@998-p.com to express your interest in such opportunities.

Our Commitment to You

The Thank You, Professionals Program is more than just rewards. It is about:

  • Acknowledging your contribution to the professional ecosystem.
  • Empowering you to influence and co-create future-ready solutions.
  • Helping you generate sustainable value — for yourself and for your community.

At 998-P, we see professionals not just as users but as partners in building a stronger, smarter, and more equitable professional world. This program is our promise to recognize, reward, and grow with you.

Together, let us create the future of professional excellence!
